Object modeling 对象模型化

Object modeling is the process of designing the objects or classes through which an object-oriented application examines and manipulates some service. Numerous modeling techniques are possible; the Cocoa development environment does not recommend one over another.

对象模型化，是指设计对象或类的过程，使得面向对象的应用程序可以检验并操作一些服务。许多模型化技术均是可行的；但 Cocoa 开发环境不建议交叉使用。

Typically, the characteristics of a class should make sense in context. Aspects such as the names of the class itself, its variables, and its methods should be recognisable to a nonprogrammer who is familiar with the service being modeled. Exactly what classes you use in your application, what attributes each class has, and what the relationships are between the classes may depend on the way you present information to the user and how you expect the user to interact with your application.

通常来说，一个类的特征应当在上下文中有实际意义。诸如类名，变量以及方法，应当易于被熟悉模型化服务的非编程人员辨识。尤其是应用程序中使用什么类，每个类拥有什么属性，以及类之间的什么关系可能会决定展示信息给用户的方式，以及期望用户如何与你的应用程序交互。

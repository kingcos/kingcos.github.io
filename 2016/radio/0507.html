Weekly Address: Happy Mother’s Day From President Obama

每周演讲：来自奥巴马总统的母亲节祝福

返回

Overview：
概览：
WASHINGTON, DC — In this week’s address, President Obama recognized all mothers in celebration of this upcoming Mother’s Day, including First Lady Michelle Obama.
华盛顿特区－本周演讲中，在即将到来的母亲节，奥巴马总统赞赏了所有的母亲，包括第一夫人，Michelle Obama。
The President emphasized that we should give all parents the support they need by providing paid maternity and paternity leave, sick leave, accommodations for workers who are pregnant, good health care, affordable child care, flexibility at work, equal pay, and a decent minimum wage.
总统强调，我们应当在带薪产假和陪产假，病假，孕工的住所，健全的医疗保健，可负担起的儿童护理，灵活的工作，同工同酬，以及合适的最低工资等方面为所有的父母提供必要的支持。
Underscoring the importance of these issues, the President called on Congress to give meaning to Mother’s Day by taking action on these items.
为强调这些问题的重要性，总统呼吁国会在这些议项上赋予母亲节（更多）意义。

The audio of the address and video of the address will be available online at www.whitehouse.gov at 6:00 a.m. ET, May 7, 2016.
演讲的音频和视频将在 2016 年 5 月 7 日于美国东部时间上午 6:00 发布在 www.whitehouse.gov。


Remarks of President Barack Obama
贝拉克·奥巴马总统的演讲
Weekly Address
每周例行演讲
The White House
白宫
May 7, 2016
2016 年 5 月 7 日


Text：
正文：

Hi, everybody.
大家好。
In our house, everybody knows that President is only the third-most important job in the family.
在我们家，每个人都知道总统只（不过）是这个家庭中第三重要的职责。
So this weekend, I’m going to take a little extra time to say thank you to Michelle for the remarkable way she does the most important job: being a mom.
所以本周末，我计划花一点额外时间来特别感谢 Michelle（，因为）她（在家里）承担着最重要的职责：身为一位母亲。
And I’m going to give extra thanks to my mother-in-law for the role model she’s always been to Michelle and the countless selfless ways in which she’s helped Michelle and me raise Malia and Sasha.
并且我（也）计划特别感谢我的岳母，因为她总是作为 Michelle 的楷模，并且帮助我和 Michelle 抚养 Malia 和 Sasha。
I am incredibly lucky to have women who help me raise, love, and look after our girls.
我是如此地幸运，有（这两位）母亲帮助我抚养，爱戴，并照顾我们的孩子。

I hope you’ll also take a moment to say thank you to the women in your life who love you in that special way mothers do.
我希望大家也花点时间感谢一生中的母亲，她们以特别的母爱的形式爱着你。
Biological moms, adoptive moms, and foster moms; single moms, grandmoms and godmothers; aunts and mentors – whomever you think of when you think of Mother’s Day.
亲生母亲，继母，或是养母；单身母亲，祖母或是教母；姨母，或是师母－任何当你想起母亲节所想起的。
Or take a moment, like I will, to remember the moms who raised us, whose big hearts sustained us, and whom we miss every day, no matter how old we get.
或者花一点时间，像我（计划的）一样，想想（曾）抚养我们的妈妈，那些用宽大的心胸包容我们的妈妈，那些无论我们如何长大（却）每天挂念的妈妈。

Giving flowers is always a good idea.
给（妈妈）几束鲜花（译者注：carnation：康乃馨）永远都不失为一个好主意。
But I hope that on this Mother’s Day, we’ll recommit ourselves to doing more than that: Through deeds that match our words, let’s give mothers the respect they deserve, give all women the equality they deserve, and give all parents the support they need in their most important roles.
但我希望在这个母亲节，我们将再次承诺比这些做的更多：言行合一，让我们给予母亲们所应得的尊重，给予所有母亲们所应得的平等，并给予所有在为人父母（这一）最重要的时期所需要的帮助。

That includes paid maternity and paternity leave, sick leave, accommodations for workers who are pregnant, good health care, affordable child care, flexibility at work, equal pay, and a decent minimum wage.
这包括带薪产假和陪产假，病假，孕工的住所，健全的医疗保健，可负担起的儿童护理，灵活的工作，同工同酬，以及合适的最低工资。
We ask our mothers to do more than their fair share of just about everything.
如果说分享应是公平的，我们（却）几乎向母亲索取了一切。
Making sure they’re treated fairly is the least we can do.
（而）确保她们被公平的对待是我们起码要做到的。

The idea of setting aside a Sunday in May for our mothers became an official holiday with a Congressional resolution a little more than 100 years ago.
100 多年以前，为我们的母亲在五月的某个周日设立（节日）的想法通过了国会决议，成为了官方节日。
They did it on May 8 – the same day we’ll celebrate Mother’s Day this year.
他们在五月八日所做－和今年庆祝母亲节（的日子）是同一天。
If Congress can make a holiday, surely they can back it up with the things that give it meaning.
如果国会（既然）能通过一个节日，（那么）他们一定（采取措施）使其赋予（更多）意义。
After all, that’s what my mother taught me.
最后，这是我的母亲（曾）教导我的。
I couldn’t just say I was going to do the right thing, or say I agreed with it on principle.
我不能空谈自己要做正确的事情，或者（只是）说原则上我同意正确的事情。
I had to actually do it.
我必须脚踏实地践行好事。

So this Mother’s Day, say thank you.
所以这个母亲节，（向妈妈）说一句谢谢。
Say, “I love you.”
说一句，”我爱你”。
And let’s make sure we show that gratitude and appreciation through acts of respect throughout the year.
并让我们承诺在每一天用实际行动表达我们的感恩与感激。
No one deserves that more than our moms.
没有人比妈妈更值得我们这样做。

Happy Mother’s Day, and have a great weekend.
母亲节快乐，顺祝周末愉快。

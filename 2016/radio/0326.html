Weekly Address: Defeating ISIL

每周演讲：打击 ISIL

返回

Overview：
概览：
WASHINGTON, DC — In this week’s address, President Obama offered his thoughts and prayers to the people of Belgium and to families of the victims of the terrorist attacks in Brussels, including at least two Americans.
华盛顿特区－本周演讲中，奥巴马总统提出其想法，并向比利时人民和布鲁塞尔恐怖袭击受害者家属祈祷，（受害者）其中包括至少两名美国人。
He reiterated our support of Belgium as a close friend and ally of the United States, especially in the fight against terrorism.
他重申（给予）作为美利坚盟友的比利时的支持，尤其在对抗恐怖主义（方面）。
The President emphasized that defeating ISIL remains our top military, intelligence, and national security priority.
总统强调，打击 ISIL 仍是我们的最高军事，情报，以及国家安全的优先事项。
He reminded us that as we move forward in this fight, we must use the power of our example as a Nation that is open to refugees escaping ISIL’s violence, refuses ISIL’s hateful and violent propaganda, and rejects any attempt to stigmatize Muslim-Americans.
他提醒我们，当我们推进这场斗争，我们必须利用作为一个国家的榜样力量，开放逃离 ISIL 暴力的难民，拒绝 ISIL 充满仇恨和暴力的宣传鼓动，并拒绝一切污辱穆斯林美国人的企图。
Together, we will not abandon our values and way of life – we will succeed and the terrorists will fail.
总之，我们将不会放弃我们的价值观和生活之道－我们将赢得胜利，而恐怖分子终将失败。

The audio of the address and video of the address will be available online at www.whitehouse.gov at 6:00 a.m. ET, March 26, 2016.
演讲的音频和视频将在 2016 年 3 月 26 日于美国东部时间上午 6:00 发布在 www.whitehouse.gov。


Remarks of President Barack Obama
贝拉克·奥巴马总统的演讲
Weekly Address
每周例行演讲
The White House
白宫
March 26, 2016
2016 年 3 月 26 日


Text：
正文：

This week, our hearts are with the people of Belgium, as terrorist attacks claimed the lives of more than thirty people.
本周，我们心系比利时人民，因为恐怖分子的袭击造成了超过 30 人丧生。

Yesterday, we learned that at least two Americans were killed.
昨天，我们得知至少两名美国人遇害。
We pray for their families and loved ones.
我们为他们的家人以及所深爱的人祈祷。
At least fourteen Americans were injured.
有至少 14 名美国人受伤。
And we pray for their full recovery – along with everyone else affected by these attacks.
我们也祈祷他们完全康复－与这次袭击中的其他人一同（完全康复）。

Earlier this week, I called the Prime Minister of Belgium and offered him our full array of support in bringing to justice any terrorists involved in planning or aiding this unconscionable attack on innocent men, women, and children.
本周早些时候，我与比利时首相通电，并向其提供我们的所有支持，审判那些参与到袭击无辜的人们和孩子的所有恐怖分子。
Belgium is a close friend and ally of the United States.
比利时是美利坚的密友，亦是盟友。
And when it comes to our friends, America has their back.
当其与我们结盟，美国便是他们的后盾。
Especially as we fight the scourge of terrorism.
尤其当我们面对恐怖主义的迫害。

More broadly, we’re going to continue to root out and defeat ISIL.
更为广泛地来说，我们计划继续打击并根除 ISIL。
We’ve been taking out ISIL leadership, and this week, we removed one of their top leaders from the battlefield – permanently.
我们已经找出 ISIL 的领导层，而且本周，我们在战场上干掉其一名顶层领导人－永久地。
A relentless air campaign – and support for forces in Iraq and Syria who are fighting ISIL on the ground – has allowed us to take approximately forty percent of the populated territory that ISIL once held in Iraq.
持续的空袭－并武力支持叙利亚和伊拉克对抗 ISIL 地面作战部队－这已允许我们收复大约四成 ISIL 在叙利亚一度占领的领土。
We’re supporting Iraqi Security Forces who are beginning to put pressure on the ISIL stronghold of Mosul.
我们正支持那些开始进攻 ISIL 位于摩苏尔据点的叙利亚安全部队。
And we will not stop until ISIL’s safe-havens are destroyed.
并且我们将不会停止，直至 ISIL 的避风港被摧毁。

We’re also working to disrupt plots against the United States and against our friends and allies.
我们同样正努力挫败袭击美利坚或我们盟友的阴谋。
A team of FBI agents is on the ground in Belgium supporting the investigation.
一支由 FBI 探员组成的队伍正在比利时地面协助调查。
We’ve ramped up our intelligence cooperation so that we can root out ISIL’s operations.
我们已经加紧情报合作，以便我们可以根除 ISIL 运作。
And we constantly review our homeland security posture to remain vigilant against any efforts to target the United States.
我们也一直评审我们的国土安全态势，以保持警惕，抵御一切目标美利坚的企图。

ISIL poses a threat to the entire civilized world.
ISIL 对人类文明世界构成了威胁。
That’s why we’ve been leading a truly global coalition that will be vital to our success.
这就是为什么我们已组织一个真正全球化的联盟，我们的成功与此密不可分。
Secretary Kerry is leading an international effort to bring the Syrian civil war to an end, a critical piece of restoring stability to that war-torn part of the world.
国务卿克里正组织一个国际行动，以结束叙利亚的内战，重回稳定的重要部分，在这个世界上饱受战争破坏的地方。
And next week, dozens of world leaders will come here to Washington for a summit focused on nuclear security.
而且在下周，许多世界领袖将来到华盛顿（参与）峰会，焦点是核安全。
We’ll use that opportunity to also review our joint efforts against ISIL, and to make sure the world remains united in this effort to protect our people.
我们将利用此次机会同样评审我们抗击 ISIL 的共同努力，并确保世界在此举上，仍团结一致以保护我们的子民。

As we move forward in this fight, we have to wield another weapon alongside our airstrikes, our military, our counterterrorism work, and our diplomacy.
当我们推进这场斗争，我们必须利用另一种武器，与我们的空袭，与我们的军队，与我们的反恐工作，以及我们的外交一同（使用）。
And that’s the power of our example.
而这就是我们的榜样力量。
Our openness to refugees fleeing ISIL’s violence.
我们对于逃离 ISIL 暴力的难民的开放。
Our determination to win the battle against ISIL’s hateful and violent propaganda – a distorted view of Islam that aims to radicalize young Muslims to their cause.
我们对于赢得对抗 ISIL 充满仇恨和暴力的宣传鼓动的决心－一个扭曲视角的伊斯兰教，目的是颠覆年轻穆斯林事业。
In that effort, our most important partners are American Muslims.
在这场行动中，我们最重要的伙伴是美国的穆斯林。
That’s why we have to reject any attempt to stigmatize Muslim-Americans, and their enormous contributions to our country and our way of life.
这就是为什么，我们必须拒绝任何侮辱穆斯林美国人，以及（污辱）他们对于我们国家和生活之道的巨大贡献的企图。
Such attempts are contrary to our character, to our values, and to our history as a nation built around the idea of religious freedom.
这种企图违背我们的性格，（违背）我们的价值观，也（违背）我们作为一个以宗教自由信念建立的国家历史。
It’s also counterproductive.
这同样是适得其反的。
It plays right into the hands of terrorists who want to turn us against one another; who need a reason to recruit more people to their hateful cause.
恐怖分子想要让我们正中其圈套，将我们内部分裂；他们需要一个理由来招募更多人加入他们充满仇恨的事业。

I am a father.
我是一位父亲。
And just like any other parent, the awful images from Brussels draw my thoughts to my own children’s safety.
而就像其他的父母一样，布鲁塞尔的惨痛印象使我对自己孩子的安全引起重视。
That’s also why you should be confident that defeating ISIL remains our top military, intelligence, and national security priority.
这同样也是为什么你应当确信，打击 ISIL 仍是我们的最高军事，情报，以及国家安全的优先事项。

We will succeed.
我们将会成功。
The terrorists will fail.
恐怖分子将会失败。
They want us to abandon our values and our way of life.
他们想让我们放弃我们的价值观和生活之道。
We will not.
我们决不会。
They want us to give in to their vision of the future.
他们想让我们屈服于他们的未来。
We will defeat them with ours.
我们将击败他们。
Because we know that the future belongs not to those who seek only to destroy – but to those who have the courage to build.
因为我们知道，未来不属于那些寻求破坏的人－而是（属于）有勇气建立的人。

Thanks.
谢谢。

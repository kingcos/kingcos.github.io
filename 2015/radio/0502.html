Weekly Address: Ensuring Every Child Gets a Great Education

每周演讲：确保每个孩子都能享有良好的教育

返回

Overview：
概览：

WASHINGTON, DC – In this week’s address, the President reiterated his commitment to expanding access to education, and to spreading the joy of reading to more children and young adults.
华盛顿特区－本周演讲中，总统重申了扩大（儿童）受教育机会的承诺，并把阅读的快乐传递给更多的孩子和年轻人。
Earlier this week the President announced two new efforts that, building on the progress already made by his ConnectEd initiative, will do just that: a challenge to mayors, libraries, and school leaders to help every student get a library card; and commitments from libraries and major publishers to provide more than $250 million in free e-Books for low-income students.
本周早些时候，总统宣布了两个新的努力（方向），以他的连通教育倡议的成果为基础，将会实施：一个对于市长，图书馆，和学校的领导们的挑战：去帮助每个学生获得一张借书证；并且图书馆和主要的出版社承诺为低收入家庭的学生提供超过 2.5 亿美元免费电子书。
In his address, the President also previewed his upcoming commencement speech at Lake Area Tech, in Watertown, South Dakota, where he will discuss his plan to make two years of community college as free and universal for every American as high school is today.
在他的演讲中，总统也预告了他即将参加位于南达科他州沃特敦的湖区技术学院（举办）的毕业典礼演讲，在那里他将探讨计划把两年制的社区大学（学费降至）免费并像当今的高中一样普及给每个美国人。
The President is working to ensure every child has the access to the education and resources they need to be successful.
总统正为确保每一个孩子（都能够）拥有接受教育的机会以及他们获得成功（所需的）资源而努力。

The audio of the address and video of the address will be available online at www.whitehouse.gov at 6:00 a.m. ET, May 2, 2015.
演讲的音频和视频将在 2015 年 5 月 2 日于美国东部时间上午 6:00 发布在 www.whitehouse.gov。


Remarks of President Barack Obama
贝拉克·奥巴马总统的演讲
Weekly Address
每周例行演讲
The White House
白宫
May 2, 2015
2015 年 5 月 2 日


Text：
正文：

Hi everybody.
大家好。
I’m speaking with you from the DC Public Library in Anacostia, where I just met with a group of promising middle school students.
我正在安那考斯迪亚的特区公共图书馆做这个演讲，在这里我刚刚遇到了一群有前途的中学生。

We spent some time talking about their lives, and how we all care about their success – and how that starts with a good education.
我们花了一些时间谈论他们的生活，和我们如何关心他们的成功——以及（孩子们的成功）如何以好的教育为开端。

So one thing I announced here in Anacostia is a new project by libraries and major publishers to provide more than $250 million in free e-Books for low-income students.
所以我在安那考斯迪亚这里宣布了一件事情：（关于）图书馆和主要的出版社承诺为低收入家庭的学生提供超过 2.5 亿美元免费电子书的新项目。

We also issued a challenge to mayors, libraries, and school leaders to help every student get a library card, so they can expand their horizons in a place like this.
我们同样向市长，图书馆，和学校的领导们发出一个挑战，去帮助每个学生获得一张借书证，这样他们就可以像在这里一样拓宽他们的视野。

It’s all part of our ConnectED initiative to connect 99% of America’s students to high-speed Internet.
把 99% 的美国学生连通至高速互联网（这些）都是我们连通教育倡议的一部分。
Because no matter who you are, where you live, or how much money you’ve got, you should be able to access the world’s knowledge and information just like anyone else.
因为不论你是谁，住在哪里，你的收入几何，你应当能够像其他任何人一样访问全世界的知识和信息。

In a global economy, we’ve got to help ensure that everyone, of every age, in every zip code – urban and rural – has the chance to learn the skills that lead directly to a good job.
在全球化经济中，我们必须确保每个人，任何年纪，任意地点——（不论身处）城市或者农村——有机会去学习可以立即获得好工作的技术。

That’s also why I’ve put forward a plan to make two years of community college as free and universal for every American as high school is today.
这也是为何我提出一个计划，把两年制的社区大学（学费降至）免费并像当今的高中一样普及给每个美国人。
It’s something I’ll talk about in my commencement address next week at Lake Area Tech, in the small town of Watertown, South Dakota.
这件事情，我将在下周即将参加位于南达科他州的沃特敦小镇的湖区技术学院（举办）的毕业典礼演讲中谈谈。
It’s a community college with a graduation rate that is nearly twice the national average.
这（所学校）是一个毕业率为国家平均水平近两倍的社区大学。
They’re proving that a great education can be within everyone’s reach.
他们正在（努力）证明，好的教育每个人都触手可及。

All of us have a responsibility to not only make sure our own children have pathways to success but that all children do.
我们每个人都有责任不仅确保我们自己的孩子拥有成功之路，而是所有的孩子都（公平）享有。
And a great education is the ticket to a better life like never before.
并且良好的教育是就是通往以往从未有过的美好生活的门票。
Making sure all our kids receive one is the surest way to show them that their lives matter.
确保我们所有的孩子都能拥有一张，这是一个最可靠的方式向他们展示生命的意义。
And it’s the smartest way to prove to them that in communities like this, and in a country like ours, we believe in opportunity for all.
并且，这是最好的方式向他们证明在这样的社会，以及在我们这样一个国家，我们相信机会对于每个人都是公平的。

Thanks, and have a great weekend.
感谢大家（倾听），周末愉快。

reiterate v./adj.重申
expand v.扩张
mayor n.市长
initiative n.倡议，首创精神
low-income 低收入
upcoming adj.即将到来的
commencement n.开始；毕业典礼
promising adj.有前途的
urban adj.城市的
rural adj.农村的
pathway n.路，径

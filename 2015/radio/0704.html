Weekly Address: Have a Safe and Happy Fourth of July

每周演讲：祝大家过一个祥和快乐的国庆节（美国独立纪念日）

返回

Overview：
概览：

WASHINGTON, DC – In this week’s address, the President wished everyone a happy Fourth of July.
华盛顿特区－本周演讲中，总统祝愿大家国庆节快乐。
He honored the individuals who, throughout the history of America, have struggled and sacrificed to make this country a better place, from our Founding Fathers, to the men and women in uniform serving at home and overseas.
他向这些个人致敬，在美国历史上为了祖国抗争和牺牲的人们，开国元勋，在国内和海外服役的人们。
The President asked that on this most American of holidays we remember the words of our founders, when they declared our independence and that all are created equal, and that we continue to protect that creed and make sure it applies to every single American.
总统希望，在这个大多数美国人的节日中，我们铭记当开国元勋宣告美国独立和宣布每一个人生来平等时（曾说）的话，（希望）我们坚守信念并永存于每一个独立的美国人。
And finally, he wished good luck to the U.S. Women’s National Team competing in the World Cup Final this weekend.
在最后，他祝愿美国国家女足队在本周末的世界杯决赛中夺冠。

The audio of the address and video of the address will be available online at www.whitehouse.gov at 6:00 a.m. ET, July 4, 2015.
演讲的音频和视频将在 2015 年 7 月 4 日于美国东部时间上午 6:00 发布在 www.whitehouse.gov。


Remarks of President Barack Obama
贝拉克·奥巴马总统的演讲
Weekly Address
每周例行演讲
The White House
白宫
July 4, 2015
2015 年 7 月 4 日


Text：
正文：

Hi everybody.
大家好。
Happy Fourth of July, everybody.
祝大家国庆节快乐。
Like many of you, Michelle, Sasha, Malia, and I are going to spend the day outdoors, grilling burgers and dogs, and watching the fireworks with our family and friends.
和你们一样，我和米歇尔，萨沙，玛丽亚打算在这天（一同）出门游玩，烤烤汉堡和热狗，和我们的家人朋友一起欣赏烟火表演。
It’s also Malia’s birthday, which always makes the Fourth extra fun for us.
这天同样是玛丽亚的生日，这对于我们来说总是国庆节时的额外惊喜。

As always, we’ve invited some very special guests to our backyard barbecue – several hundred members of our military and their families.
和往常一样，我们邀请了一些特别的客人来我们的后院一起 BBQ （烧烤）－几百个军队成员以及他们的家人。
On this most American of holidays, we remember that all who serve here at home and overseas, represent what today is all about.
在这个大多数美国人的节日中，我们铭记所有在国内和海外服役的人们，因为没有他们就没有（美国的）今天（直译：他们代表着现在的一切）。
And we remember that their families serve, too.
我们也铭记他们的家庭的付出。
We are so grateful for their service and for their sacrifice.
我们十分感激他们的服役甚至他们的牺牲。

We remember as well that this is the day when, 239 years ago, our founding patriots declared our independence, proclaiming that all of us are created equal, endowed by our Creator with certain unalienable rights including the rights to life, liberty, and the pursuit of happiness.
我们同样铭记，在 239 年前的今天，我们的开国元勋宣告美国独立，宣布我们每个人生来平等，声明我们的造物主赋予那些不可剥夺的权利，包括生命权，自由权和追求幸福的权利等。

A couple of centuries later, we have made ourselves into a big, bold, dynamic, and diverse country.
几个世纪过去，我们已经将祖国建设成一个强大，不屈，充满活力且多样化的国家。
We are of all races, we come from all places, we practice all faiths, and believe in all sorts of different ideas.
我们是各个种族，我们来自五湖四海，我们拥有所有信仰，并且我们相信各种奇思妙想。
But our allegiance to this declaration – this idea – is the creed that binds us together.
但是我们的效忠这个宣言－这个想法－（它）是我们团结一致的信念。
It’s what, out of many, makes us one.
它就是，摆脱分裂，归于统一。

And it’s been the work of each successive generation to keep this founding creed safe by making sure its words apply to every single American.
并且它是每一代人所一同坚守信念的成果，他们使得这些言语植根于每一个独立的美国人。
Folks have fought, marched, protested, even died for that endeavor, proving that as Americans, our destiny is not written for us, but by us.
人们为了（这项）事业打仗，示威，抗议，甚至付出了生命，向美国人证明，我们的命运并不是指挥着我们，而是掌握在自己手里。

We honor those heroes today.
现在，我们缅怀这些英雄。
We honor everyone who continually strives to make this country a better, stronger, more inclusive, and more hopeful place.
我们向每一个仍然努力使得这个国家更加美好，更加强壮，更加包容，更加充满希冀的人致敬。
We, the people, pledge to make their task our own – to secure the promise of our founding words for our own children, and our children’s children.
我们，人民，要视他们的任务为己任－为我们自己的子子孙孙坚守我们的开国宣言。

And finally, what better weekend than this to cheer on Team USA – good luck to the U.S. Women’s National Team in the World Cup Final!
最后，还有什么比助威美国国家女子足球队更完美的周末－祝愿她们在本周末的世界杯决赛中夺冠！

Thanks, everybody.
感谢大家（倾听）。
From my family to yours, have a safe and happy Fourth of July.
来自我们全家的祝福，祝大家过一个祥和快乐的国庆节。

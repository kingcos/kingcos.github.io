Weekly Address: Creating New Pathways of Opportunity for Americans Like You

每周演讲：为像你一样的美国人创造新的机遇之路

Overview：
概览：

WASHINGTON, DC – In this week’s address, the President spoke to his priority of growing the economy and opening new avenues of opportunity for hardworking Americans.
华盛顿特区－本周演讲中，总统谈到他的首要使命是发展经济并为努力奋斗的美国人敞开新的机遇之门。
While the United States has already made economic progress, with more than 12 million new private sector jobs created over the past five years, there’s still more to be done.
当然，美国在经济方面已经取得了进步，在过去的五年中新增了超过 1200 万个私营行业工作岗位，（但）路还很长。
That’s why the President has continued to press for strong, high-standard trade agreements that are good for American workers and good for American businesses.
这就是总统继续施压（通过）强大，高标准的贸易协议的原因，（因为）它受益于美国工人和美国企业。
And it’s why his Administration has partnered with mayors and governors across the country on issues such as minimum wage and paid leave that impact hardworking Americans.
而且这（也）是其政府联合全国各地的市长和州长（解决）问题的原因，例如（提高）最低工资和带薪休假等（实实在在）影响（那些）努力奋斗的美国人（的问题）。
The President discussed impactful initiatives like these in his address before the Conference of Mayors on Friday.
在周五的市长会议前，总统（也）在演讲中探讨了以上这些有影响力的举措。

The audio of the address and video of the address will be available online at www.whitehouse.gov at 6:00 a.m. ET, June 20, 2015.
演讲的音频和视频将在 2015 年 6 月 20 日于美国东部时间上午 6:00 发布在 www.whitehouse.gov。


Remarks of President Barack Obama
贝拉克·奥巴马总统的演讲
Weekly Address
每周例行演讲
The White House
白宫
June 20, 2015
2015 年 6 月 20 日


Text：
正文：

Hi everybody.
大家好。
As President, I spend most of my time focused on what we can do to grow the economy and grow new pathways of opportunity for Americans like you to get ahead.
作为总统，我把大部分时间专注于如何发展经济并为像你一样的美国人创造新的机遇之路。

And we’ve made progress.
并且已经卓有成效。
More than 12 million new private sector jobs in the past five years.
在过去的五年中新增了超过 1200 万个私营行业工作岗位。
More than 16 million Americans who’ve gained health insurance.
超过 1600 万美国人获得了医疗保险。
More jobs creating more clean energy.
更多的就业岗位，更多的清洁能源。
More kids graduating from high school and college than ever before.
从高中和大学毕业的孩子也比以往更多。

But in a relentlessly-changing economy, we’ve got more work to do.
但是在持续变化的经济中，我们的路还很长。
And one of the things we should be doing, for example, is rewriting the rules of global trade to benefit American workers and American businesses.
例如，其中我们应做的一件事就是，重新制定全球贸易规则使我们的工人和企业受益。
I think we should write those rules before China does.
我想我们应该赶在中国之前着手制定。
That’s why I’ve been working with Congress to pass new, 21st century trade agreements with standards that are higher and protections that are tougher than any past trade agreement.
这就是为什么我敦促国会通过新的 21 世纪贸易协议，（其中包含）比以往贸易协议更高的标准和更严格的保护（条例）。

I believe it’s the right thing to do for American workers and families, or I wouldn’t be doing it.
我相信为美国的工人和家庭这样做是值得的，否则我不会这样做。

I believe it’s what will give us the competitive edge in a new economy, or I wouldn’t be doing it.
我相信这将让我们在新经济中（保持）竞争优势，否则我不会这样做。

Now, several Members of Congress disagree.
（但）现在，（还有）一些国会议员不认同。
That’s why it’s still tied up there, along with a lot of other good ideas that would create jobs.
这就是为什么它仍然搁置在这里，伴随着许多其它可以创造就业岗位的好点子。
And eventually, I’m optimistic we’ll get this done.
但我很乐观，（相信）最终我们将完成它。

But America doesn’t stand still.
而美国并没有无所作为。
That’s why, on issue after issue where Congress has failed to act, my administration has partnered with mayors and governors across the country to advance economic priorities that most working families in America are in favor of right now.
这就是为什么，国会已经否决一个接一个的问题，（但）政府已经联合全国各地的市长和州长推进现在美国的大多数工薪家庭支持的经济合作。

And we’ve had success.
我们已经取得成功。
Over the past couple years, 17 states and six major cities have raised the minimum wage for their workers.
在过去的几年，17 个州和六个主要城市已经为其工人上调最低工资。
19 cities have enacted paid sick days, and five states have enacted paid sick days or paid family leave.
19 个城市已经实施带薪病假，并且已经有五个州实施带薪病假或带薪探亲假。
34 states have increased funding for quality Pre-K.
34 个州已经上调学前教育基金。
And 19 cities and states have signed up for our new TechHire initiative to train workers for the high-wage, high-skill jobs of tomorrow – the kind of jobs that new trade deals would help create.
并且有 19 个城市或州已经签署了新的 TechHire 计划，来训练工人在未来获得高薪高技能工作－新的贸易协议将会有利于创造这种类型的工作。

Some of these victories have been small.
（可能）这些成果很微小。
Some have been quiet.
（可能）这些成效没有回响。
But they’ve added up to a big difference for working families across America.
但它们积累起来对于全美国的工薪家庭意味着巨大改变。
And that’s what matters to me.
这就是对于我来说最重要的。
Because it matters to you.
因为它对关系到你。
On Friday, I talked about these initiatives and more in a speech to the U.S. Conference of Mayors.
周五，我谈到过这些举措，在对美利坚国会议员的演讲中讲得更加详细。
Check it out at WhiteHouse.gov.
（大家可以）点击 WhiteHouse.gov 查看（详细内容）。
Some of it might matter to your city.
其中一些可能关系到你的城市。

Thanks, and have a great weekend.
感谢大家（倾听），周末愉快。

relentlessly adv.持续地

pay leave 带薪休假
tie up 占用；包扎，绑好；阻碍
